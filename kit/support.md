# KiT Support

**KiT** is a minimalistic interval trainer for iPhone and Apple Watch.

## Getting help

If you have a question, run into a problem, or want to request a feature, email
**karl@mcclendo.net** and I'll get back to you.

## Frequently asked questions

### How do I create a workout?

Tap one of the slots on the home screen, set your warm-up, work, rest, and
cool-down durations and the number of rounds, then tap **Start**. Give it a name
if you like; it's saved automatically.

### Can I use KiT while my phone is locked?

Yes. The timer keeps running with the screen off, plays your sound and haptic
cues, and shows the current interval countdown on the Lock Screen.

### Does it work with Apple Watch?

Yes. Starting a workout can launch the companion Watch app, which mirrors the
timer on your wrist and records the session to Apple Health so it counts toward
your Exercise ring.

### Does KiT save my workouts to Apple Health?

With your permission, completed sessions are recorded to Apple Health. You can
review or revoke this access anytime in the Health app under **Sharing -> Apps**.

### How do I turn sounds, voice, or haptics on or off?

Each workout has its own toggles for voice announcements, beeps, and haptics in
the workout editor.
