# KiT Privacy Policy

_Last updated: July 4, 2026_

KiT ("the app") is an interval-training timer developed by Unremarkable Games.
This policy explains how the app handles your information. The short version:
**KiT does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. Everything stays on
your device and in Apple Health.**

## Information the app does not collect

KiT has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party
tracking SDKs. The app does not send your information to us or to anyone else.
There are no servers involved; the app has no network component for your data.

## Workouts you create

The workouts you build (durations, round counts, names, and sound/haptic
settings) are stored only in the app's private storage on your device. They are
not uploaded anywhere. Deleting the app removes this data.

## Apple Health (HealthKit)

With your permission, KiT records your completed interval sessions to Apple
Health so they count toward your Activity rings, and on Apple Watch it reads
heart rate and active energy during a session to log the workout. This data is
managed by Apple Health under your control and your device's privacy settings.

- KiT does **not** use Health data for advertising or marketing.
- KiT does **not** share Health data with any third party.
- KiT does **not** transmit Health data off your device.

You can review or revoke KiT's Health access at any time in the Health app under
Sharing -> Apps, or in Settings -> Privacy & Security -> Health.

## Notifications and Lock Screen

KiT uses the system Alarms/Live Activity feature to show your interval countdown
on the Lock Screen. This runs on-device and involves no personal data collection.

## Children

KiT does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new
